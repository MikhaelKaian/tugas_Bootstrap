THE REAL MADRID CAPTAIN WAS NAMED BEST PLAYER OF THE YEAR.

On the 17th of October 2022, Karim Benzema was crowned king of football. Following a spectacular 2021/22 campaign, France Football awarded the Real Madrid captain the 2022 Ballon d'Or. The gala, held in Paris, was attended by an extensive delegation from our club, headed by president Florentino Pérez. Benzema finished ahead of Mané, De Bruyne and Lewandowski in the voting carried out by 180 international journalists. Having collected the prize from Zidane, Benzema reflected: "Holding this trophy is such an honour, it's a childhood dream come true. It's an individual award, but a team one too, and I think this is the Ballon d'Or of the people. Thank you to all of Madrid, to the players, because without them this would have been impossible, to the fans for always motivating me to give my best on the pitch, and to the president as well". The Frenchman received the accolade after taking to Real Madrid to their Decimocuarta European Cup, a 35th LaLiga title and a 12th Spanish Super Cup, topping the scoring charts in every competition with 15, 27 and 2 goals respectively. Benzema also played a key role in his country clinching the Nations League trophy and had already been named UEFA Best Men's Player.

THE BELGIAN WAS NAMED BEST GOALKEEPER OF THE 2021/22 AT THE BALLON D'OR GALA.

The Ballon d'Or 2022 gala crowned Thibaut Courtois as the greatest goalkeeper in the world. The Belgian clinched the Yashin Trophy, awarded by France Football. The Real Madrid keeper beat Bounou, Alisson, Ederson, Maignan, Mendy, Neuer, Oblak, Trapp and Lloris in the voting. At the ceremony, held at the Théâtre du Châtelet in Paris, the goalkeeper described his joy at receiving the prize: “It's an honour to win this trophy with all the fantastic goalkeepers that are here. I'd like to thank my Real Madrid teammates, it's a pleasure to be part of this incredible team, who had an unbelievable season. I'd like to thank my family and my fiancée as well. It's always important to have your family close by, especially when things aren't going so well".

New adidas and Real Madrid casual wear line inspired by Marvel's Avengers out now

For the first time, adidas has launched a limited edition Marvel's Avengers casual wear line for Real Madrid. The new clothing collection features a range of Marvel Universe superheroes including Captain America, Iron Man, Hulk, Black Widow, Thor and Black Panther. Kroos and Asensio, alongside the basketball team's Llull, Tavares, Causeur, Yabusele and Randolph, plus women's first-team players Ivana Andrés and Teresa Abelleira, will star in the Avengers launch campaign. The men's first team will wear the Captain America t-shirt during the pre-match warm-up and the Avengers hoodie as they take to the pitch at the Bernabéu ahead of Sunday's game with Girona. The women's team will do the same when they take on Sevilla. Meanwhile, Real Madrid's Basketball team will show off the full range at their League clash with Fuenlabrada and their Euroleague encounter with Emporio Armani Milan. They will also wear the line during their official training sessions. This is adidas' first collection with a shared theme for all sections of the club and it is on sale now at adidas.com and in official Real Madrid stores.

The Spanish Super Cup will be held in Riyadh from January 11th until 15th

The Spanish Super Cup will be staged in Riyadh (Saudi Arabia) from 11 to 15 January 2023, the Spanish Football Federation has announced. Reigning champions and LaLiga winners Real Madrid will meet Copa del Rey runners-up Valencia in the semi-finals. Should they progress to the final, Carlo Ancelotti's side would go on to face the winners of the Betis-Barcelona clash. The semi-finals will kick off on 11 and 12 January and the final on 15 January. All matches will be played at the King Fahd Stadium in Riyadh, which can accommodate more than 60,000 spectators.
